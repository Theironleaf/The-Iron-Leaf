Research & Evidence

THE
EVIDENCE
HIERARCHY.

Every claim at The Iron Leaf is rated against a five-tier evidence scale. We never present opinion as fact — and we always explain where the evidence is strong, where it's emerging, and where it's uncertain.

This is how we distinguish ourselves from every other health and fitness platform. We show our work. We acknowledge uncertainty. We change our recommendations when the evidence changes.

★★★★★
STRONG EVIDENCE

Systematic reviews and meta-analyses of multiple randomised controlled trials

★★★★
GOOD EVIDENCE

High-quality randomised controlled trials with consistent findings

★★★
MODERATE EVIDENCE

Well-designed observational research and cohort studies

★★
LIMITED EVIDENCE

Small studies, preliminary research, or expert consensus

★
EMERGING EVIDENCE

Early-stage studies, hypotheses, and promising but unconfirmed research
