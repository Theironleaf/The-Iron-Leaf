How we collect, use, and protect your information when you use The Iron Leaf.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

The Iron Leaf ("we", "us", "our") is an educational platform for sustainable human performance, covering whole-food plant-based nutrition, strength and conditioning, mobility, recovery, longevity, and trail running. This policy explains what information we collect when you use theironleaf.com, why we collect it, and how it is handled.

We built The Iron Leaf as an education-first platform — the large majority of our content (guides, research summaries, pillar pages, the library) is freely accessible without ever creating an account or submitting personal information. This policy primarily concerns the limited situations where you choose to share information with us directly.

Information We Collect

We collect information in the following situations:

  • When you contact us — through the contact form, we collect your name, email address, topic, and message content.
  • When you join our newsletter — we collect your email address to send the weekly letter you've requested.
  • When you enquire about programmes — coaching, corporate wellness, or speaking enquiries may involve additional details you choose to share, such as your goals or organisation.
  • Automatically, through standard web analytics — like most websites, our hosting and analytics tools may collect technical information such as browser type, device type, approximate location (city/country level), and pages visited, to help us understand how the platform is used and improve it.

We do not require account creation to access any educational content, free guides, or the research library.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to enquiries sent through the contact form
  • Send the weekly newsletter to subscribers who have opted in
  • Evaluate and respond to coaching, corporate wellness, or speaking enquiries
  • Understand aggregate usage patterns to improve site content and structure
  • Maintain the security and proper functioning of the platform

We do not use your information to make automated decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects, and we do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information in limited circumstances:

  • With service providers who help us operate the platform (such as email delivery or hosting providers), under obligations to protect your information
  • If required by law, regulation, or valid legal process
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of The Iron Leaf, our users, or others

Cookies & Tracking

The Iron Leaf may use essential cookies necessary for basic site functionality, and may use standard analytics tools to understand aggregate visitor behaviour (such as which pages are most read, or how people navigate between pillars). We do not currently use cookies for third-party advertising or cross-site tracking.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time; doing so should not affect your ability to read educational content on the platform.

Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions and programme enquiries for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to and resolve them, and newsletter subscriber information for as long as you remain subscribed. You may request deletion of your information at any time using the contact details below.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export the personal information we hold about you, and to withdraw consent (such as unsubscribing from the newsletter) at any time. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

Children's Privacy

The Iron Leaf is intended for a general adult audience and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time as the platform evolves. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page when changes are made. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, reach us at hello@theironleaf.com, or use our contact page.
