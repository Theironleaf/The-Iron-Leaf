THE TEN
FRAMEWORKS.

Not marketing language. The actual intellectual spine of everything taught at The Iron Leaf — ten structured systems, each governing a specific dimension of sustainable human performance.

Why Frameworks

SYSTEMS,
NOT RULES.

Every recommendation on this platform traces back to one of these ten frameworks. They are how we ensure that whether one article exists or ten thousand, everything feels like it belongs to one coherent educational philosophy.

A framework is different from a rule or a tip. Rules tell you what to do. Frameworks teach you how to think about a domain — so that when circumstances change, you can adapt intelligently rather than following instructions blindly.

Each framework below governs a specific pillar of the platform — strength, mobility, longevity, nutrition, and beyond — while the tenth, the Body Literacy Framework, sits above all of them as the master philosophy unifying everything we teach.

These are living systems. They are refined as evidence evolves, but their structure remains stable — which is precisely what allows the platform to grow into thousands of pieces of content without losing coherence.
